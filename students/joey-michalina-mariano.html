Biography

Joey Michalina Mariano is an electronic musician known for pioneering the 8bit guitar sound. The 8bit foot controller has been viewed on youtube 89,000 times. Joey won the open spark competition and had the song "dmg guitar" played through two 13 feet tall tesla coils. Along with her musical endevours she is also an avid coder and enjoys building things from 1's and 0's.
